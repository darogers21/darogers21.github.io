Places We've Beans

For my project, I wanted to make something for you. I'm slightly late for your birthday but I thought I'd make a page of some of the amazing places that we've been lucky enough to see together.

These are only just the beginning of all the places we will go.

Rob Roy Track, New Zealand

The defining moment of our relationship? I think so. Summiting what has been called the most epic hike in history* in record breaking time, galvanised the two of us into what many have described as the great relationship in history.

The moments when you flipped me off behind my back where so loving and tender that I knew that was the womas of my dreams and set a course for our future together.

Bruny Island, Australia

The Neck. The meeting of two seas, two beaches, two souls. It was here that we agree to leave the rest of the world behind and start our new lives living in a remote lighthouse in Tasmania

However, when you discovered there was no wifi in lighthouse, the incentive to do it for the gram disappeared along with your participation in this plan.

Yosemite National Park, USA

Another defining accomplishment in our expanding relationship resume. The most handsome couple to complete the Mist trail in matching Kanken's^.

Not only was it one for the record books, it kicked off our great tour of America's west and reminded me that there's no other in this world that I could enjoy spending 2 weeks in a car with.

Horseshoe Bend, USA

Although it caused you to have a severe panic attack (and perhaps the mere sight of it again will induce another) I included Horseshoe Bend as it is the visual symbol of the way you turned my life around.

Emerging from University dazed and disenchanted, meeting you changed my perspective on life, gave me new direction and helped me flow into the gushing stream of a man I am today.

Wanaka, New Zealand

A town of panoramic views, iconic trees and brilliant cinemas. Our few days in Wanaka were so momentus that thinking about who I was before Wanaka... it's like trying to say hello from the other side.

How could we ever forget experience driving into town, being whipped back and forth on endless switchbacks until our necks snapped and we were forced to pay attention to stunning views in front of us.

It is truly the only place that could rival your eyes in beauty.

420 Blaze! Ha ha just kidding.

Remember when we saw that epic beaver-like creature? Or when we stayed at in that super sketchy town and I lost my credit card? Or when you friends got married on top of the mountain?

Yeah Colorado was pretty great.
